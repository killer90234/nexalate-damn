Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How quickly can you start my project?

We can typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks after contract signing and requirements finalization. For urgent projects, we offer express start options within 3-5 business days.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) services including bug fixes, updates, security patches, and 24/7 technical support. We also provide training to your team if needed.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites: 2-4 weeks, Complex web applications: 2-6 months, Mobile apps: 3-8 months, Enterprise software: 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during project planning.

Do you work with clients outside Gujarat?

While we specialize in serving Gujarat businesses, we do work with clients across India and internationally. We use modern collaboration tools to ensure seamless communication regardless of location.
